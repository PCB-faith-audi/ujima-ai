🛡️ Guardian Agent Boundaries
  • Guardian triages — it never gives a final approval or denial
  • Loans above KES 15,000 are always escalated to a human officer
  • 3 or more risk flags trigger mandatory human review

⚖️

Risk Triage Only — Not a Loan Decision. Guardian provides a risk summary to support your loan officer's decision. It does not approve or deny loans. All final decisions are made by qualified human loan officers at your SACCO. You may request human review at any time by dialling *#733#.

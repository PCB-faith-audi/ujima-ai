ETHOS Framework

Five guardrails that govern every AI-assisted loan decision

Core Governance
E

Equity — Who Might We Exclude?

Before every model update, we identify 3 vulnerable groups that may be systematically disadvantaged: women vendors, Busia County residents, and seasonal farmers. Exclusion triggers an immediate model audit.

T

Transparency — Show Your Reasoning

Every risk assessment comes with a plain-language explanation. No black-box outputs. Guardian must justify every flag in language a village elder would understand.

H

Household Impact

Before flagging a "high concern" assessment, the system models the household food security impact of denial — factoring in children's ages, school calendars, and harvest timing.

O

Oversight — Humans Stay In Charge

An immutable audit trail assigns human accountability to every decision. AI recommends. Humans decide. No exceptions for loans above KES 50,000 or applicants with children under 5.

S

Sovereignty — Your Data Stays in Kenya

All member data is processed and stored exclusively on AWS Africa (Cape Town). Zero data is shared with external parties. Kenya DPA 2022 compliance is mandatory, not optional.

TRACK Bias Audit

Forensic analysis of our training data and model behaviour — conducted quarterly

Quarterly Audit
T
Training Data
What % represents informal traders?
Current training corpus is 38% informal trader data — below our 50% target. Remediation: partnership with Kenya National Bureau of Statistics for Q3 data injection. Urban-centric bias risk acknowledged and flagged.
R
Representation
Does taxonomy include shea butter traders?
Occupation taxonomy updated to include 47 informal trade categories including shea butter, chapati vendor, mkokoteni operator, and miraa trader. Gender-neutral labels enforced throughout.
A
Amplification
Does model amplify historical exclusion of women?
Red team test confirmed: female market vendor in Busia with identical cash flow to male formal employee received equivalent risk score after bias correction patch (v2.1.4). Monthly gender parity checks scheduled.
C
Counterfactuals
What if market vendor had identical cash flow to formal employee?
Counterfactual test result: with identical cash flow, market vendor now receives equivalent or better score due to harvest-cycle income spike modelling. Pre-v2.0 gap of 18 points has been eliminated.
K
Kill Switch
Busia County mandatory human review
All applications from Busia County are flagged for mandatory human review regardless of AI score. This override is hardcoded and cannot be modified without Elders Council approval. Active since January 2025.

OASIS Data Stewardship

How we collect, store, protect, and delete your data — Kenya DPA 2022 compliant

DPA 2022
✋Opt-In Consent

No data is collected without explicit opt-in. Consent is given in Swahili, Luhya, or English. You choose exactly which data trains the model.

✓ Active
🎭K-Anonymity Anonymisation

All data is anonymised to k=5 depth, preventing re-identification even in small villages. Names, phone numbers, and IDs are never stored together.

✓ k=5 enforced
☁️Data Sovereignty

All data is stored exclusively on AWS Africa (Cape Town). No data crosses into European or US data centres. Data flow is publicly audited.

✓ AWS af-south-1
⏱️180-Day Auto-Delete

Non-essential metadata is automatically deleted after 180 days. Loan history (needed for SACCO records) is retained only with your explicit consent.

✓ Automated
🔐End-to-End Encryption

All USSD and SMS channels use AES-256 encryption. Data in transit and at rest is fully encrypted. Security audits conducted every 6 months.

✓ AES-256
🚫Zero External Sharing

Your data is never sold to, shared with, or accessible by any external party including Silicon Valley AI companies, advertisers, or government agencies without court order.

✓ Contractually binding

PRIDE Human Oversight Loop

The five checkpoints that keep humans in control of every consequential decision

Human-in-Loop
P

Pause Points — When AI Must Stop

Mandatory human review is triggered for: loans above KES 50,000; applicants with children under 5; any application from Busia County; 3+ risk flags from Guardian; or any mention of debt collectors or loan sharks.

*#733# → Instant human override
R

Review Cadence — Regular Fairness Audits

Quarterly fairness audits are conducted with the SASRA compliance team. Results are published publicly on this page. Any sub-county with a 30%+ approval rate drop vs. baseline is investigated within 48 hours.

I

Interpretability — Explain Like a Village Elder

Every risk flag must be explained in plain language. No technical jargon. Example: "Your income dips during school fee season — we need 3 months of buffer before committing to this repayment amount."

D

Disagreement Rights — Challenge Any Decision

Every member has the right to challenge any AI assessment. Dial *#123# or visit your SACCO branch. The challenge must be reviewed by a human officer within 5 business days, with a written explanation provided.

*#123# → Request formal review
E

Elders Council — Community Governance

Governance council composition: 3 SACCO managers, 2 women market vendors, 1 fintech regulator (CBK-nominated), 1 agricultural extension officer. Council meets quarterly and holds veto power over model updates.

Your Rights as a Member

Under Kenya DPA 2022 and SASRA regulations, you have six enforceable rights

DPA 2022
👁️

Right to Access

Request a full copy of all data Ujima AI holds about you at any time. Response within 14 days.

✏️

Right to Correct

If any data about you is wrong, you have the right to have it corrected or deleted immediately.

🚫

Right to Withdraw Consent

Withdraw your data consent at any time. Dial *#733# or visit your SACCO branch.

🤝

Right to Human Review

Any AI assessment can be escalated to a human officer. No AI decision is ever final alone.

📢

Right to Complain

Formal complaints go to the Office of the Data Protection Commissioner of Kenya within 30 days.

🗑️

Right to Erasure

Request complete deletion of all your data. Processed within 30 days. SACCO legal records excluded.

HORIZON Impact Scan

10-year ecosystem impact analysis — projected across three generations

Long-term Ethics
H — Historical Harm

Does this replicate colonial extraction of African financial data? Active safeguard: all data sovereignty contractually guaranteed to remain in Kenya.

O — Opportunity Cost

Will automation erode chama savings culture? Mitigation: Scout Agent actively reinforces group savings behaviour and chama participation.

R — Ripple Effects

Loan access has been correlated with 12% improvement in school attendance in Kakamega County pilot (2024 internal study).

I — Intergenerational

Designed to build generational wealth through harvest-cycle financial literacy passed from parents to children, not dependency on AI.

Z — Zero-Sum Check

Who profits: Local SACCO (sustainability). Who suffers: None, if ethical design is maintained. Profit extraction is capped at SASRA-regulated rates.

O — Open Futures

System designed to preserve human agency. Exit ramps are built at every step. Members can opt out completely without penalty to their loan eligibility.

N — Non-Human Impact

Server carbon cost monitored quarterly. AWS Cape Town runs on 80%+ renewable energy. Carbon offset program active for remaining 20%.
